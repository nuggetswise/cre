COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease Agreement is made and entered into as of March 1, 2024, by and between:

Landlord: Riverside Properties LLC  
Address: 1200 Market Street, San Francisco, CA 94103

Tenant: Velocity Logistics Inc.  
Address: 782 Industrial Way, Oakland, CA 94607

1. Premises  
Landlord hereby leases to Tenant the commercial office space located at 1200 Market Street, Suite 300, San Francisco, CA 94103 (the "Premises").

2. Term  
The lease term shall commence on April 1, 2024, and shall continue for a period of five (5) years, expiring on March 31, 2029.

3. Rent  
Tenant shall pay a monthly base rent of $12,000 due on the first day of each month. Late payments shall incur a fee of $200.

4. Use  
The Premises shall be used for general office purposes, including logistics operations, and shall not be used for any unlawful or industrial activity.

5. Renewal Option  
Tenant shall have one (1) option to renew the lease for an additional term of three (3) years at a rate to be negotiated in good faith.

6. Termination Clause  
Either party may terminate this agreement with 120 days written notice in the event of a material breach that remains uncured.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Lease Agreement as of the Effective Date.
